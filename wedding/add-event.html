What happens next

Behind the scenes
1

Activity tagged — wedding only

Only wedding activities are accepted here. Real estate and special events are handled on their own separate portals.

2

Time synced to couple's local timezone

A bride in New York sees New York time. A couple in Dubai sees Dubai time. No manual conversion needed on either end.

3

Synced to your Google Calendar

The entry appears on Ms. D'Ette's own Google Calendar automatically, already tagged and sorted — no re-entry needed.

4

Confirmation sent to couple

The couple receives a summary with the date, time in their local timezone, and the activity details.

In this prototype, "Add to calendar" triggers a confirmation toast only. Timezone conversion and Google Calendar sync would be wired in the real build.
